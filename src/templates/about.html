About our company

Our company is built on the foundations of security. Being a banking application, it has to be as secure as possible, which if not followed can lead to the loss of private user data

We use an innovative new system called keystroke dynamics. This includes taking in the typing patterns from a particular client, analyzing them and finding a pattern. Then the computer can always identify if the same client is typing or an intruder into the account is typing.
